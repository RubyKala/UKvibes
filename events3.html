Asan Bird Sanctuary

The Bird Sanctuary was established in 1967 as a manmade wetland where rivers Yamuna and Asan converged. This area attracts a large number of migratory birds

Benog Wildlife Sanctuary

Situated in Mussoorie, It is known for providing shelter to the endangered species such as mountain quails, leopards, deers, foxes and some red billed blue magpie.
